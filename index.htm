About Angel Investor Course

This course is for those who are interested in startups and investing in them!

Angel Investor Course is organized by “Technovate and Sabah Angel Club” - the first Angel Investor’s Club in Azerbaijan. This course is for those who
has stable annual income and able to invest between $3,000 and $50,000 annually. This amount of investments are considered to be small and usually
done by angel investors at very early stage of the startup.

Startup investment is considered to be highest reward investment tool in the world. It is the only way where you can exponentially grow (ten to
thousand times) your wealth in a very short amount of time. But those investments comes with risks as well. This course will give you theoretical
knowledge you need to make best possible decisions.
